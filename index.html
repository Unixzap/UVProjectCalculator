OPTIONAL COMMUNITY SUPPORT

Support UV Project Calculator Pro

UV Project Calculator Pro Community Edition is available without a required payment or subscription. If the application saves you time, improves your pricing, or helps your UV printing business, an optional contribution can help support continued development.

A Note from Don Youngner

Creator of UV Project Calculator Pro

I originally created this calculator because I needed a better way to understand the real cost of my own UV printing projects. As the application grew, I realized it could help other makers and small businesses price their work with more confidence.

Your optional support helps cover website expenses, testing, documentation, new printer profiles, and continued improvements. There is absolutely no obligation to contribute—the Community Edition remains fully usable whether you support the project or not.

Thank you for using UV Project Calculator Pro and for being part of its development.
